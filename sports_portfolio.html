At National CineMedia, he translated that sports-audience fluency into cinema — earning the 2019 NCM Panel of Excellence, one of six from 240 professionals nationally.

Super Bowl / Patriots Playoff Special Sections

Jeff was an early developer of the Patriots playoff and Super Bowl special section franchise at the Boston Globe, building tiered rate structures that rewarded schedule commitment while capturing revenue from both pre-game and post-game coverage windows.

Patriots · 2008 · Perfect Season Run

Patriots Playoffs 2008 — Special Section Framework

AFC Championship + Super Bowl + Potential Perfect Season Commemorative

For the 2008 Patriots playoff run — which ran through the AFC Championship (San Diego @ New England, Jan 20) and potential Super Bowl XLII (Feb 3, Arizona) — Jeff built a tiered, commitment-based rate structure with incentives that rewarded advertisers for booking early and across multiple dates. The structure was ahead of its time: rate cards rewarded combinations of 1 Sunday + multiple daily repeats, with special pricing for the post-Super Bowl commemorative issue and the "Perfect Season" section contingent on a New England win.

The Globe planned to print ~15,000 additional copies distributed in Arizona Jan 31–Feb 3. If the Patriots won, a Sunday Feb 10 "Perfect Season Commemorative" was added, requiring a commitment alongside the Feb 4 post-game issue. Rates ranged from $252/PCI (1 Sunday + 1 daily) to $410/PCI (1 Sunday + 6 dailies). Color at 20% additional. Minimum buy: 31.5 inches.
